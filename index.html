ABOUT

Version Beta is a technical conclave comprising of Central India's largest hackathon, workshops, and seminars. It is dedicated to spread the spirit of application, innovation, and creativity among the next generation of developers and engineers. With participation from colleges across the nation, the 36-Hour long hackathon provides an opportunity for the students to collaborate and conceptualize a real-world problem. The workshops and seminars facilitated by industry experts add up to the experience. Version Beta holds the key to overall development in the social and technical aspects of an individual.
